What are Snowball (SNOB) tokens?
Snowball (SNOB) is a governance token rewarded to stakers on the protocol.

How do I earn Snowball (SNOB) tokens?
Earn Snowball tokens by either depositing sPGL tokens or SNOW-AVAX LP tokens into the IceQueen contract
